# Privacy Policy for Frases de Rajoy

**Effective date:** 2026-05-13
**Last updated:** 2026-05-13

This Privacy Policy describes how HTBR ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares personal information when you use the mobile application Frases de Rajoy ("the App"). HTBR is a trading name used by an individual developer (autónomo) based in Spain. For service-provider identification purposes under Spain's Ley 34/2002 (LSSI-CE), you can reach the developer at the contact email below.

Frases de Rajoy is a single-screen mobile application that delivers a daily local notification with a humorous quote attributed to the Spanish politician Mariano Rajoy, accompanied by an image. The App operates entirely on your device: it does not connect to any server, does not require an account, and does not include any analytics, advertising, or tracking technologies.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

The data controller responsible for any personal information processed in connection with the App is:

- **Name:** HTBR
- **Country:** Spain
- **Contact email:** htbr.app@gmail.com

You can contact us at any time using the email above for any matter related to this Privacy Policy.

## 2. Personal information we collect

**We do not collect any personal information through the App.**

In particular, the App:

- Does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any identifier.
- Does not connect to any backend server operated by us or by any third party.
- Does not include analytics, crash-reporting, advertising, attribution, or tracking SDKs of any kind.
- Does not request access to your contacts, location, microphone, camera, photo library, calendar, health data, biometric data, Bluetooth, or any other sensitive resource.
- Does not use cookies (the App is a native mobile application; it has no web component).

The only data stored on your device is two non-personal application preferences:

| Preference | Type | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| `are_notifications_paused` | Boolean (true/false) | Remembers whether you have paused the daily notification |
| `is_dark_mode_override` | Boolean (true/false) | Remembers your manual override of the light/dark theme |

These preferences are stored locally on your device using AndroidX DataStore — a small file-based preference store. On Android, the file lives inside the App's private storage directory; on iOS, the same library writes to a file inside the App's sandbox. In both cases, the data never leaves your device, is not transmitted anywhere, and cannot be linked to your identity.

If you grant the App permission to display notifications, the operating system retains a record of that permission grant. That record is managed by the operating system (Android or iOS) and is not accessible to us.

If you believe we have collected information not disclosed here, please contact us at htbr.app@gmail.com.

## 3. How we use your information

Because we do not collect personal information, we do not use it for any purpose.

The two non-personal preferences described in Section 2 are used solely to make the App behave as you configured it (whether notifications are paused; which theme is displayed). They are not used for profiling, advertising, or any automated decision-making.

We do not use personal information for automated decision-making that produces legal or similarly significant effects on you.

## 4. Who we share your information with

We do not share any personal information with any third party, because we do not collect any.

The App has no third-party processors, no analytics providers, no advertising networks, and no backend infrastructure operated by us. The App functions entirely offline once installed.

Distribution of the App itself is handled by Apple (App Store) and Google (Google Play). When you download or update the App, those companies process information about you (such as your Apple ID or Google account, device model, and download events) under their own privacy policies, independently of us. We do not receive personal information from them beyond aggregated, non-identifying download statistics.

**We do not sell or share your personal information.** This is true under both the GDPR's interpretation of "sale" and the California Consumer Privacy Act's broader definition of "sale or share" (which includes sharing for cross-context behavioral advertising). There is nothing to opt out of, because no such transfer takes place.

## 5. How long we keep your information

We retain no personal information because we collect none.

The two non-personal preferences described in Section 2 remain on your device until you:

- Toggle them through the App's interface, or
- Clear the App's data through your operating system's settings, or
- Uninstall the App.

Uninstalling the App removes all of its locally stored data from your device.

## 7. Security

The App stores its two non-personal preferences in the standard storage areas provided by the operating system, which apply the platform's default isolation (Android per-app data directory, iOS app sandbox). Other apps on your device cannot read this data.

Because the App makes no network requests, no data leaves your device, and therefore there is no transmission to secure.

No method of digital storage is 100% secure. While we believe the architecture described above offers strong protection by avoiding data collection altogether, we cannot guarantee absolute security in all circumstances.

## 8. Children's privacy

The App is intended for a general audience aged 14 and over. This age threshold corresponds to the digital-consent age set by Spain's Ley Orgánica 3/2018 (LOPDGDD), Article 7, for the processing of personal data on the basis of a minor's own consent.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under this age. Because the App does not collect personal information from anyone — child or adult — this position is enforced by the App's design (no analytics, no accounts, no network calls) and not only by policy.

If you are a parent or guardian and have a concern about your child's use of the App, please contact us at htbr.app@gmail.com.
